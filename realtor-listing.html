Listing Description

Welcome to the desirable Hamptons community! This home is steps from parks, ponds, and walking trails, and sits across from the pond on a wide street with a comfortable sense of space between homes. This fully finished 5 bedroom home is located in an established neighborhood with schools and amenities in place, including Bessie Nichols Public K-9 and Sister Annata Brockman Catholic K-9, both a short walk away. The main floor features updated custom cabinetry, while the fully finished basement offers 2 bedrooms, a spacious living area, and a full bathroom with heated floors. Upstairs offers an expansive bonus room, a laundry room, and three bedrooms, including a lovely primary retreat with a walk-in closet and a spa-like 5-pc ensuite with double sinks, a soaker tub and a separate shower. Clean, well cared for, and tastefully updated throughout, this move-in ready home also provides quick access to Anthony Henday Drive, the Whitemud, shopping, dining, and everyday amenities. Simply unpack and enjoy. (33777896)
